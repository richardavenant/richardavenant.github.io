About Me

As a student at UCT, my years of studies have shaped the individual I am today by honing and enhancing many of my unrefined skills. I am confident and outgoing in nature. I excel at interpersonal communication and thus I am effective at problem solving within this sphere. My natural and interest driven command of the English language has resulted in an ability to exercise inducement.

Given the nature of my tertiary studies and personal strengths I am eager to explore career paths that will allow me to put these strengths to their most effective use. My current ambition is to gain experience in a multitude of sectors in order to discover what best suits my skillset. In my belief the best way to do so is to apply myself to the best of my ability to any given task, thus pushing the limits of my capabilities. I take my responsibilities seriously and attempt to make the best out of every situation. As my tertiary studies draw to a close I wish to continue working diligently towards a new goal.

Currently, I am working towards honing my knowledge in marketing, specifically social media and other online variants. I have made rapid progression in this field through a number of internships and personal interest. I thoroughly enjoy working in the industry and thus have decided to further my participation in it with the aim of curating a career path in marketing.
